Solve These Common Problems

Lost sales and frustrated customers due to stockouts and backorders

Report: Backorders

The Backorders report joins sales orders, customers, order lines, and products, then filters by date range and optional customer name while using the Backorder flag to list only affected lines with quantities and line totals for immediate fulfillment follow-up.

Tied-up cash and emergency supplier runs due to reordering too late or too often

Report: Reorder

The Reorder report filters products by supplier/category and calculates QtyShort as (ReorderPoint - QuantityOnHand), optionally limiting to items under reorder, so purchasing can target the exact shortages without overbuying.

Late payments and cash-flow surprises due to unclear payment status by order

Report: SalesPaySts

The SalesPaySts report groups orders by PaymentStatus and sums related line totals over a selected date range, making it easy to see unpaid or pending revenue and prioritize collections.

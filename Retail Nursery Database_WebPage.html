How It Solves These Problems

Backorders Report: The Backorders report joins orders, customers, order lines, and products, then filters by order date range and customer name and can optionally show only backordered lines, giving you a prioritized fulfillment and call-back list.
InvValue Report: The InvValue report calculates RetailValue, CostValue, and GrossMarginValue from UnitPrice, Cost, and QuantityOnHand and lets you filter by supplier and category, so purchasing decisions are based on real dollars on the ground.
Delivery Report: The Delivery report filters RequestedDate between prompted start and end dates and outputs customer-linked delivery contact and address fields, producing a clean schedule you can use for routing and confirmations.

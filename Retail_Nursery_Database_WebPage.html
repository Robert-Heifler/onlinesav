Solve These Common Problems

Stockouts and backorders cause lost sales, frustrated customers, and constant fire-drills at the counter.

Report: Backorders

The Backorders report joins orders, customers, order lines, and products, then filters by date range and customer name while surfacing the Backorder flag per line (Qty, UOM, LineSubtotal, LineTotal). This lets staff instantly find what is owed, who is waiting, and the revenue at risk so they can prioritize fulfillments and customer callbacks.

Inventory levels drift out of sync (on-hand vs real), leading to overbuying, missed reorders, and dead stock tying up cash.

Report: Reorder

The Reorder report lists products by supplier/category and calculates QtyShort as (ReorderPoint - QuantityOnHand), with an optional 'Only Under Reorder' filter. This turns replenishment into a repeatable weekly process: sort by supplier, see shortages immediately, and build purchase orders without guessing.

Cash flow is unclear because payment status is scattered across orders, making it easy to miss follow-ups and under-collect.

Report: SalesPaySts

The SalesPaySts report groups order totals by PaymentStatus and includes OrderDate and SalesOrderId, making unpaid vs paid totals visible for any date window. This creates a clean AR workflow: filter to 'Unpaid' (or similar), print/call list, and measure collection progress over time.

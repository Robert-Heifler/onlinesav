Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Access Wizard.

What do I get in the Free Version?

The Free Version lets you create up to 5 forms per computer. You get full access to all core features including AI-powered table generation, automatic form creation, and relationship management. This is perfect for small projects and learning how Access Wizard works.

What happens when I reach the 5-form limit?

When you try to create your 6th form, Access Wizard will prompt you to upgrade to Pro. Your existing forms continue to work perfectly—you simply can't create new forms until you upgrade. Nothing is deleted or disabled.

What does the Pro Version include?

The Pro Version ($39 one-time payment) gives you unlimited form creation, unlimited databases, commercial use license, and lifetime updates. Enter your license key once and it stays unlocked forever—no subscriptions, no recurring fees.

Can I use Access Wizard for my business?

Yes! The Pro Version includes a commercial use license. The Free Version is for personal projects only. If you're creating databases for clients or using it in your business operations, you'll need the Pro Version.

Do I need programming skills?

No! Access Wizard is designed for non-programmers. Simply describe what you need in plain English (like "I need a database to run my nursery business") and the AI generates the tables and forms automatically. No coding required.

What versions of Microsoft Access are supported?

Access Wizard works with Microsoft Access 2016, 2019, 2021, and 365 on Windows 10 or Windows 11. You can also use the free Microsoft Access Runtime if you don't have the full version of Access.

How does Access Wizard save me money?

Hiring a Microsoft Access developer typically costs $50-$120 per hour. Even a simple database with 5 forms would cost $300-$700 in professional fees. Access Wizard automates this entire process for a one-time $39 payment (or free for up to 5 forms). You save hundreds or thousands of dollars.

Can I edit the databases after creation?

Yes! The databases are standard Microsoft Access files (.accdb). You can open them in Access and customize anything you want—add fields, modify forms, create new reports, or hire a developer for advanced customizations. You have complete control.

How do I get my Pro license key?

Visit the purchase page, pay $39 via PayPal, and you'll be redirected to a page with your license key. Copy the key, open Access Wizard, and click the "Activate Pro" button to unlock unlimited features instantly.

Is there a subscription or recurring fee?

No! The Pro Version is a one-time $39 payment. You pay once and own it forever, including all future updates. No subscriptions, no monthly fees, no hidden costs.

Can I use my license on multiple computers?

Your license is tied to one computer. If you need to use Access Wizard on multiple machines, you'll need a separate license for each. The Free Version works on any computer but is limited to 5 forms per machine.

What if I need help or have technical issues?

Contact us at info@onlinesav.com or use our contact form. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. Pro users get priority support.

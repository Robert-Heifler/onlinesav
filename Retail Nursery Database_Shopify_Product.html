Nursery Sales, Inventory & Delivery Manager (Microsoft Access)

Nursery Sales, Inventory & Delivery Manager is a ready-to-use Microsoft Access database built for plant nurseries and garden centers that need clean order history, accurate inventory, and dependable delivery scheduling.

Instead of juggling spreadsheets, handwritten tickets, and separate customer lists, you track everything in one system:

  • Customers: contact info, address details, preferred contact method
  • Products: SKU, category, plant type, variety, sizes, supplier, unit price, cost, reorder point, quantity on hand
  • Sales orders: order date, requested date, payment method, payment status
  • Order lines: quantities, pricing, discounts, tax, totals, and backorder status

What you get: 17 built-in reports that answer the questions you get every week. Quickly filter by date range, customer name, supplier, category, product name, payment status, and more using simple prompt boxes.

  • Backorders: see open backordered lines by customer and date
  • Reorder: flag items under reorder point and calculate how many to buy
  • Delivery: print a delivery schedule with requested dates and full drop-off details
  • Inventory Value: instant retail value, cost value, and gross margin value by item
  • Sales analytics: top customers, top products, sales by category, sales by payment method/status

Ideal for: solo operators and small teams who want a practical system staff can learn fast, with no monthly fees.

Requirements: Microsoft Access on Windows, or the free Microsoft Access Runtime. All tables, queries, and reports are fully editable, so you can tailor fields, labels, and layouts to match your nursery's terminology.

Tip: start by importing your product list and setting reorder points, then begin entering orders the same day. Within a week you'll have accurate backorder lists, cleaner purchasing, and fewer missed deliveries.

Note: This is a desktop database for Microsoft Access users. No monthly fees.

Download the free Microsoft Access Runtime if you don't have Microsoft Access installed.

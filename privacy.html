Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information when you use Access Wizard Databases and the onlinesav.com website.

1. Information We Collect

Website Usage

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymous information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Geographic location (city/country level)
  • Referring website

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

Contact Forms

When you contact us through our support form, we collect your name, email address, and message content solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Payment Information

All payments are processed securely through PayPal. We never see or store your credit card information. PayPal provides us with transaction confirmation only.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to support inquiries and provide customer service
  • To process purchases and deliver database files
  • To improve our website and understand user behavior (via anonymous analytics)
  • To send download links and order confirmations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely through Formspree, a trusted third-party service. Payment processing is handled entirely by PayPal with industry-standard security measures.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and usage tracking
  • PayPal: Secure payment processing
  • Formspree: Contact form processing

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking (use browser plugins or privacy settings)

To exercise these rights, contact us via our support form.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

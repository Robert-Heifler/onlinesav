SMS Opt-In Policy & Consent Disclosure

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Access Wizard Databases / OnLineSav.com

About This Program

Access Wizard Databases (operating at OnLineSav.com) sends informational and promotional SMS messages to small business owners and tradespeople who have explicitly opted in by replying to an initial outreach text message. We sell Microsoft Access database templates designed for service businesses including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, and related trades.

How Recipients Opt In — Via Text Reply

All recipients enter this messaging program through a two-message, reply-based opt-in process. No links or promotional content are sent until the recipient replies to Message 1, constituting their explicit consent.

1
Message 1 — Outreach (no links, no promotional content):
We send an initial SMS introducing ourselves and asking a single question relevant to the recipient's business. Example: "Hi, this is Bob from Access Wizard — we build job tracking software for HVAC businesses. Are you still using spreadsheets to track your jobs? Reply STOP to opt out."
2
Recipient replies — this reply is the opt-in:
The recipient's reply to Message 1 constitutes their express consent to receive follow-up messages. No reply = no further contact. Recipients who reply STOP at any time are immediately removed.
3
Message 2 — Follow-up (sent only after reply):
After the recipient replies, we send a single follow-up message containing a link to a free tool or relevant product page on OnLineSav.com. All messages include opt-out instructions.

Message Content & Frequency

Messages relate to job-tracking database software for small trades businesses — informational tips, free tools, and one-time-purchase product links. Recipients receive a maximum of 2 messages per outreach sequence. We do not send recurring automated blasts or subscription-based campaigns.

Opt-Out Instructions

Reply STOP to any message at any time to be immediately removed from all future outreach. No further messages will be sent after a STOP reply. Reply HELP for assistance or contact us directly at the address below.

Rates & Carriers

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and plan. Access Wizard Databases is not responsible for carrier charges incurred by recipients.

Privacy

Phone numbers and any information shared via SMS are used solely to respond to the recipient's inquiry and deliver the follow-up message they opted into. We do not sell, share, or transfer phone numbers to third parties for marketing purposes.

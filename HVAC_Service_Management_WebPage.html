Action Reports

Action Reports

Contained within the reports included with your purchase, these Action Reports are the real solution solvers.

UnpaidInv: The UnpaidInv report pulls every invoiced job that has not been paid, sorted by customer and invoice date. You can filter by customer name or date range so you always know exactly who owes what and how long it has been sitting. Run it every Monday morning and your collections calls write themselves.
OpenJobs: The OpenJobs report shows every job without a completion date, flagging anything past its scheduled date as OVERDUE in plain sight. Filter by technician to see who has a backlog building and which customers are waiting longest. No more end-of-week surprises about work that was never finished.
SvcDueList: The SvcDueList report lists every active customer whose next service date is coming up or already past, sorted by urgency. Enter how many days out you want to see and the report builds your call list automatically. Capture that recurring revenue before the customer calls someone else.
